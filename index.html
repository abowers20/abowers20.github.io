About

Finance student at Babson College with experience as President of the Babson Finance Association, Facilitator of SPEAR IB, Sector Lead in the Babson College Fund, Co-Founder of Babson's Scholars of Finance chapter, and Student Advisor in the Stephen D. Cutler Center for Investments and Finance.

These experiences have and will continue to prepare me for success as an incoming Investment Banking Summer Analyst at Jefferies.

All of the aforementioned commitments have required me to become an organized, driven problem-solver. I seek out opportunities for personal growth, fueled by a curious mind that is passionate about finance.
